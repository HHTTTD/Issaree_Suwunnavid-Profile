Issaree
Suwunnavid

Issaree Suwunnavid is a graduate of Mahidol University, a prestigious institution in Thailand.

Ms. Suwunnavid boasts an extensive and impressive career spanning various sectors of international business. Her current role is CEO of Knightsbridge Asia, a position she has held since 2016. She concurrently serves as CEO for Buzz Technologies Inc (USA), a Fintech company, and Senior Partner & Commercial Real Estate Consultant at Knightsbridge Law.

Previously, she demonstrated her entrepreneurial spirit by founding Prestige Asia (2005-2014), a successful Investor Relations, Public Relations, and Marketing company. Now, as a part of Knightsbridge, it has transformed to Prestige Infinity, a strong CRM tool for corporates and a luxury personal concierge to individual clients. Her leadership extends beyond the private sector. Ms. Suwunnavid served as CEO & President of Solar Energy Co., Ltd (2010-2011) and Asia CEO for Ebeling Heffernan (2009), a financial publication company (currently www.livetradingnews.com).

Her early career included experience in project management, sales, and marketing. She played a key role in the design, construction, and launch of Koi Restaurant Bangkok, a branch of the renowned LA establishment. She also held a sales and marketing position at Koll, a real estate management company.

Issaree Suwunnavid is a highly sought-after consultant due to her deep understanding of international business. She is a Key Opinion Leader frequently interviewed on financial news, real estate trends, social issues, and lifestyle topics.

Her expertise extends beyond financial matters. Ms. Suwunnavid has served as a consultant to various government agencies. She played a crucial role in promoting Thai cinema at major international film festivals like Cannes, Hong Kong Fimart, and the American Film Market for over 12 years.

Her influence spans the entertainment industry as well. She consulted for the National Federation of Thai Films Associations, securing high-profile appearances at the HuaHin International Film Festival and the Thailand International Film Destination Festival. She further solidified her place in the entertainment world by consulting for Miss Grand International, YG Entertainment Thailand Audition, and Thailand Society on Arirang TV.

Beyond her professional accomplishments, Ms. Suwunnavid is a dedicated philanthropist. She actively contributes to several charitable organizations, including:

  • Foundation for Slum Child Care, Bangkok
  • Bangkok Chef Charity
  • World Childhood Foundation, founded by Her Majesty Queen Silvia of Sweden
  • The Program of Life Giving to the Parents of AIDS-infected Babies by HRH Princess Soamsawali
  • Princess Pa Foundation, Thai Red Cross Society by HRH Princess Bajrakitiyabha

She was also a member of Zonta International District 7, Zonta Bangkok Club 5, demonstrating her commitment to empowering women and girls.

Issaree Suwunnavid's career trajectory showcases her exceptional business acumen, leadership skills, and commitment to social good. Her vast experience positions her as a leading figure in international business, with an impressive track record of success in diverse sectors.
